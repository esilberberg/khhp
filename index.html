WELCOME TO THE KENTUCKY HISPANIC HERITAGE PROJECT

The Kentucky Hispanic Heritage Project presents multimodal and multimedia data in a user-friendly interface that allows visitors to browse content as well as locate specific data efficiently. The collection encompasses qualitative and quantitative data, academic research, news and press coverage, oral history interviews, community organization information, original creative works, cultural events, and relevant public/private initiatives. We invite you to explore the collection by source type or topic.
